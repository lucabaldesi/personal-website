--- name: IoT device testbed image: images/iot_testbed.png chocolate: 96 --- Testing and validating both off-the-shelf and prototype IoT devices require an automated infrastructure. I have designed, implemented, and deployed a testbed to automatize the initialization of experiments focused on network traffic analysis. The testbed manages a WiFi network the devices are connected to. Each device is communicating on a dedicated VLAN to avoid cross-experiment interference. The devices are registered in the main server and researchers can reserve their usage and automatically start collecting their network traffic through a web interface.
