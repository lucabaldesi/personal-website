--- name: Colosseum website: https://colosseum.net chocolate: 98 --- Colosseum is a wireless network channel emulator with hardware in the loop[[1]](javascript:toggle_display('colosseum-colosseum')) sporting 256 USRP Software Defined Radios designed and released by DARPA in 2019. * Despite being a flexible network emulator, standard SDR cannot support 802.11 packet timing requirements; designed, implemented, and deployed an extension using Zylinx System-on-chip boards and OpenWiFi to fully enable Wi-Fi communications. 
A. Chaudhari and M. Braun, “A Scalable FPGA Architecture for Flexible, Large-Scale, Real-Time RF Channel Emulation,” in 2018 13th International Symposium on Reconfigurable Communication-centric Systems-on-Chip (ReCoSoC), 2018, pp. 1–8. doi: 10.1109/ReCoSoC.2018.8449390.
