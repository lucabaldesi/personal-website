Profile

I'm a post-doc researcher at Northeastern University, working on wireless networks and machine learning. I served as a research and development engineer at Mindmaze working on embedded systems and signal processing on networks. Previously, I had been a post-doc researcher at the University of Trento, Italy, where I got my PhD. degree in Computer Science.
My research interests are in the area of computer networks, IoT, graph theory, and machine learning. My expertise includes the design and development of communication systems, distributed algorithms, and embedded prototypes.
